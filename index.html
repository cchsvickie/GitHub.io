Welcome to Iron Mills Ranch

Family-run horse boarding on 20 beautiful acres in Coupeville, WA. Experience top-notch care in a serene environment.

About Us

We are a passionate family dedicated to providing exceptional horse boarding services. Located in the heart of Coupeville, Washington, our 20-acre ranch offers a peaceful setting with easy access to trails and natural beauty. Our facilities include spacious pastures, shelters, and everything your horse needs for comfort and health.

With years of experience, we ensure daily care tailored to each horse's needs, fostering a community where owners and animals thrive.

Our Services

We offer flexible boarding options to suit your needs. All plans include daily feeding, turnout, and basic care.

  • Full Board: $500/month - Includes hay, grain, stall cleaning, and use of arena and round pen.
  • Pasture Board: $350/month - Grassy pastures with shelters and daily checks.
  • Self-Care Board: $250/month - You handle feeding; we provide space and facilities.
  • Add-ons: Trailer parking ($50/month), blanketing ($30/month), or custom training sessions (inquire for rates).
  • Facilities: 130 x 70 arena, round pen, wash rack, and direct trail access.

Note: Prices are estimates; contact us for current rates and availability.
